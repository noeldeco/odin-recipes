Huge Suspicious Sausages in a Scarlet Pimpernel Sauce

Description

Self explanatory

Ingredients

  • One horse's willy
  • One large, ripe frog

Steps

  1. Cook horse's willy
  2. Make Scarlet Pimpernel sauce
    1. Take large, ripe frog
    2. Squeeze, as if wringing out a wet cloth
  3. Pour sauce on willy
